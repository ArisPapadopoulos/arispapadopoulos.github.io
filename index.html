About me

I'm a third-year PhD student at the University of Leeds, supervised by Pantelis Eleftheriou and Dugald Macpherson. Before coming to Leeds, I was down south, doing an MEng in Mathematics and Computer Science (with a specialisation on Pure Mathematics and Computational Logic) from Imperial College. My thesis was about interactions between model theory and complexity theory.
