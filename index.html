About me

I am a Novikov Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Maryland, mentored by Chris Laskowski.

Before crossing the Atlantic, I was a PhD student at the University of Leeds, supervised by Pantelis Eleftheriou and Dugald Macpherson. During my PhD, I was busy having some model-theoretic considerations in finite combinatorics, or something. Some results from my thesis can be found in the papers numbered 1,3-5 below.

In the more distant past, before taking the LNER Azuma to Leeds, I was down (up?) south, doing an MEng in Mathematics and Computer Science (specialising in Pure Mathematics and Computational Logic) at Imperial College, London. My master's thesis focused on interactions between model theory and complexity theory, or whatever.
